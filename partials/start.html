Mofowallet is free and decentralized access to FIMK and NXT


  Secure


All encryption is handled client-side. Neither your passphrase nor any of your private information ever leaves your browser.

You must always backup your highly secure passphrases. For easier daily usage we also offer an encrypted wallet file, this wallet file works in all supported browsers.

  Decentralized


Download the client and become part of the network. Or access through the web over one of our own or any of the public decentralized API servers.

While mofowallet is decentralized software it is in no way slow or unresponsive like traditional crypto currency clients.

Mofowallet ships with customized FIMK and NXT engines which support two way push/pull communications making mofowallet the fastest and most interactive way to interact with FIMK or NXT.

  Easy


Open source mofowallet is under constant development always trying to make working with FIMK and NXT easier for the beginning user.
